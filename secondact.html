WHITE DRESSES by Paul Greene

MARY.

_Oh!..._ Yes, I reckon so.

[_She is silent._

MORGAN.

He's a eddicated boy, an' he knows. Dey teaches 'im how to know everything out yander at dat college place. He sees my worf', he does. Co'se I ain't braggin', but de gals all do say ... oh, you know what dey says.

MARY.

[_Jumping up from her chair._]

Jim Matthews, you think I'd marry a ... oh, I'd ...

AUNT CANDACE.

[_Turning around._]

What's you sayin', gal?
